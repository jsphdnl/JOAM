Shruti Duge


Vocalist | Music Composer | Sound Enthusiast

About


A practitioner of the music who conveys deepest emotions through the medium of music and overtly expressive eyes

My journey in the vast ocean of music began at the age of six with learning Hindustani classical music. As a young child, the presence of music was responsible for the development of my core ideologies and deepest principles of life. I have grown with, and alongside music. As of now, my explorations in music delve into the realm of alternative forms, by fusing seemingly unconventional genres, using and mixing sounds of everyday objects, and experimenting with ways of making music. I also indulge in occasional improvised covers, both global and Indian, when I find a resonance with the song, its structure and identity. I dream of bringing the world together, as one, through my medium of music.
