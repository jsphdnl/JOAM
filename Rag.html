About


I am a music composer with an MSc. in Digital Composition and Performance from the University of Edinburgh. Besides my specialization, I'm extremely passionate about related fields such as visual arts, mobile application development, digital media, sound design and film.

My interests lie in Indian Classical Fusion, Electronic Music Production and Contemporary Music Technology. My latest release FLAIR - Carnatic Music 2.0, is a project that attempts to present Carnatic Music compositions in a modern style, by fusing them with elements of contemporary electronic music. My current goal is to present Carnatic Music in a way that would appeal to the young and modern audiences of today. My passion for technology and the has also led me to pursue projects involving mobile application development, visual design and film.
